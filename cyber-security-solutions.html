Introduction to Cyber Security Awareness

In today's digital world, cybersecurity has become a crucial aspect of our daily lives. As technology continues to evolve, so do the threats that target our data, privacy, and personal security. Cybersecurity awareness is not just about understanding these threats but also about adopting safe practices to protect ourselves and our organizations. The rise of cybercrime has led to financial losses, identity theft, and breaches of confidential information on a global scale. Therefore, educating individuals and organizations on cybersecurity measures is paramount.

Cybersecurity encompasses a wide range of practices designed to protect systems, networks, and data from cyber threats such as malware, phishing, ransomware, and social engineering attacks. Phishing attacks often disguise themselves as legitimate emails to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information. Malware, on the other hand, can infiltrate devices without user consent, causing severe damage. Ransomware encrypts data and demands payment for its release, posing a significant risk to businesses and individuals alike. Social engineering exploits human psychology to manipulate individuals into divulging confidential information.

What is Cyber Security Protection?

Cyber Security Protection refers to the practices, technologies, and measures designed to safeguard computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, theft, and damage. It encompasses a broad range of strategies to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information in digital environments.

Confidentiality

Ensuring that sensitive information is accessible only to authorized parties

Integrity

Maintaining and assuring the accuracy and completeness of data

Availability

Ensuring systems and data are accessible when needed
